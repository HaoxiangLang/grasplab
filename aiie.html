AI in Engineering Reading Group @ OntarioTech University

AI in Engineering

Reading Group

All are welcome!

The AI in Engineering Reading Group at OntarioTech University is organized to discuss recent research papers regarding Artificial Intelligence(AI) in Engineering Applications. Other topics in advanced control and engineering applications are also welcome. Our meeting format is presentations with lots of audience interaction, so please read the paper before attending the meeting.

Each presentation should be structured to answer the folllowing questions:

  • Motivation
    • What problem(s) are the author(s) addressing?
    How does it relate to engineering applicaitons?
  • Method(s)
    • How does it work?
  • Limitations
    • How does it compare to existing methods?
  • Discussions
    • What are the benefits; and what are the drawbacks?
